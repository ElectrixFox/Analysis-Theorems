Definition 2.1 Limit of a sequence
✓
#

Lean declarations

  • seq_is_limit

The limit of a sequence \(x_n\) as \(n \to \infty \) is

\[ \forall \varepsilon {\gt} 0, \exists N \in \mathbb {N}, \forall n \geq N, |x_n - l| {\lt} \varepsilon . \]
